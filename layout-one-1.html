Software Engineering Research Lab

Software Engineering Research Lab is a research laboratory that focuses on investigating various aspects of software engineering.

About - SERL

SERL@IIITA is a research lab focused on developing scalable solutions for complex software systems based on business needs.

About Software Engineering Research Lab

The (SERL) at IIIT Allahabad will conduct research on designing complex, large-scale software systems that meet business needs. The lab will cover various research domains and address industrial software engineering challenges. Collaboration with industry experts will ensure solutions are thoroughly evaluated for real-world applications. The lab will provide a platform for researchers and students to showcase their potential globally.

About - Mission & Vision

"Big thinking precedes great achievement."

About Mission & Vision

The mission of this initiative is to establish a research lab of an industrial repute through various scholarly activities such as development of software tools, establishment of special interest groups, organizing conferences, workshops, forums for open source solutions and hands-on training in order to provide reliable and repeatable results to reflect our core strategy and vision of deepening excellence.
